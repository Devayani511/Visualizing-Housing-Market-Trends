The "Golden Era" Premium

Our data shows a surprising trend: Houses between 80 and 110 years old actually command a higher premium (Avg: ~$550k) than mid-century homes built 40-70 years ago (~$435k). This suggests a strong market for historic architecture and high-quality vintage builds.

Condition vs. Valuation

The state of the home is a major multiplier. Homes in Excellent condition sell for an average of $568,074, whereas homes in Okay condition drop significantly to $318,590. This indicates that renovation provides one of the highest returns on investment in this market.
